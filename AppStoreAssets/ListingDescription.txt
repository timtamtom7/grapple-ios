# Grapple — App Store Listing Content

## App Name
Grapple

## Subtitle
Challenge Your Thinking

## Description

Grapple is your intellectual sparring partner — enter any thought, belief, plan, or argument, and it will systematically challenge you from multiple angles, helping you think more clearly before you commit.

**How it works:**
1. Enter your thought, belief, or argument
2. Grapple identifies the strongest counter-arguments across four categories: Factual, Logical, Emotional, and Practical
3. Respond to each challenge with your rebuttal
4. Receive a balanced synthesis that separates what survived scrutiny from what collapsed

**Key Features:**

🧠 **Four Challenge Modes**
- Quick: Three sharp challenges in under 60 seconds
- Standard: Five varied challenges with rebuttals
- Deep Dive: Eight+ challenges across multiple rounds
- Opposing View: AI argues the strongest case for the other side

📚 **Source Integration**
Paste source URLs and Grapple reads them to build contextually relevant challenges

✅ **Real-Time Fact Check**
Get instant fact-checking on specific claims within arguments

📱 **Beautiful, Focused Design**
Dark theme, distraction-free interface built for deep thinking

💬 **Community Feed**
Browse what others are grappling with, bookmark interesting sessions, and publish your own

📄 **PDF Export**
Export your synthesis as a professional PDF document

**Privacy First**
Your sessions are stored locally on your device. Community posts are shared anonymously.

**Who is Grapple for?**
- Anyone who wants to stress-test decisions before making them
- Students and researchers exploring complex topics
- Writers refining arguments and narratives
- Professionals preparing presentations or proposals
- Curious minds who want to think more rigorously
