# Grapple — Version 1.0 Release Notes

## Version 1.0

Welcome to Grapple — your intellectual sparring partner.

**Core Features:**
- Enter any thought, belief, or argument
- Receive multi-angle challenges (Factual, Logical, Emotional, Practical)
- Write your rebuttals and get a balanced synthesis
- Four debate modes: Quick, Standard, Deep Dive, and Opposing View
- Beautiful dark theme with distraction-free design

**Additional Features:**
- Source URL integration — paste sources for contextual challenges
- Real-time fact checking on specific claims
- Community feed — browse and publish public sessions
- Topic explorer — discover sessions by category
- PDF export of your synthesis
- iPad side-by-side layout
- Apple Watch quick grapple entry

**Free Tier:** 3 grapples per month
**Pro:** Unlimited grapples

Built with care for thinkers who want to reason better.
