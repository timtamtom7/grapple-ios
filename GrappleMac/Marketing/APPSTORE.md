### Full Description

**Think better. Argue stronger. Ship with confidence.**

GrappleMac brings the intensity of a competitive debate court directly to your Mac. It is your personal thinking partner — one that never lets a weak argument slide.

---

### How It Works

**Stage 1 — Submit Your Claim**
Enter a belief, plan, argument, or piece of writing you want to pressure-test. No matter the domain — philosophy, policy, writing, or strategy.

**Stage 2 — Face the Counter**
GrappleMac challenges your thinking with precise, structured counter-arguments across four types:
- *Factual* — What evidence is missing or wrong?
- *Logical* — Where does the reasoning break down?
- *Emotional* — Is bias clouding judgment?
- *Practical* — What fails in real-world execution?

Each argument is scored by severity so you know which challenges hit hardest.

**Stage 3 — Write Your Rebuttals**
Respond to every challenge. GrappleMac provides instant judgment on the strength of each rebuttal — strong, partial, or weak.

**Stage 4 — Get a Synthesis**
GrappleMac synthesizes the full exchange: what survived scrutiny, what collapsed, what still needs evidence, and a final verdict on the strength of your position.

---

### Features

- **Structured Debate Protocol** — Four rigorous stages modeled on academic debate format
- **Instant Feedback** — Real-time judgment on argument strength at every step
- **Challenge of the Week** — Weekly debate prompts to sharpen specific thinking skills
- **Tournament Mode** — Compete in structured tournaments and earn champion badges
- **Menu Bar Quick Access** — Launch a new Grapple directly from your menu bar
- **Full History** — Revisit past sessions, track how your thinking has evolved
- **Menu Bar Quick Access** — Start a new Grapple without opening the full app

---

### Why GrappleMac?

Most thinking tools help you *generate* ideas. GrappleMac helps you *destroy* bad ones.

The result: clearer arguments, stronger writing, and decisions you can defend.

---

### Privacy

Your debates are stored locally on your Mac. No data is ever sent to external servers. GrappleMac uses local AI inference for all argument generation.
